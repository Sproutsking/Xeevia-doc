06.5The Mechanics Underneath

Participation
With Weight.

Why must participation carry weight if trust is to remain meaningful? Because free engagement is zero-value engagement.

This is not post-to-earn. Not like-to-earn. Not a daily login bonus. Xeevia never rewards activity simply because it occurred. Every meaningful action costs EP. Participation becomes investment, not free consumption.

Earning works the other way: when your contribution helps create a valuable conversation, you share in the value that conversation generates — weighted by your reputation and demonstrated contribution, not by your account age or deposit size.

01
Deposit Funds Participation

$1 becomes 100 EP and enters the liquidity pool. This is a funding step, not an earning event. It buys the capacity to participate — nothing more.

02
EP Is Spent, Never Given Free

Publishing, commenting, replying, and proposing all cost EP. Priced engagement produces intent. Free engagement produces noise.

03
Conversations Create Value

A post, the comments it draws, the replies those generate — the whole discussion is the economic unit, not any single action within it.

04
Contribution Score Decides the Split

Reaction quality, reply depth, endorsement weight, and reputation tier determine each contributor's share — not raw activity.

05
Reputation Multiplies Every Payout

A Trusted Contributor earns more from the same conversation than an unverified account. Reputation compounds into economic weight.

06
