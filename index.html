06.5The Mechanics Underneath

Participation
With Weight.

Why must participation carry weight if trust is to remain meaningful? Because free engagement is zero-value engagement.

This is not post-to-earn, not like-to-earn, not a daily login bonus. Xeevia never rewards activity simply because it occurred. Every meaningful action — publishing, commenting, replying, proposing — costs EP. Participation becomes an investment, not free consumption.

Earning works in the opposite direction: when your contribution helps create a valuable conversation, you share in the value that conversation generates, weighted by reputation and genuine contribution. Trust comes first. Economic value is the consequence, not the bait.

01
Deposit Funds Participation

$1 becomes 100 EP and enters the liquidity pool — a funding step, not an earning event. It buys the capacity to participate, nothing more.

02

Publishing, commenting, replying, and proposing all cost EP. Free engagement produces spam — priced engagement produces intent.

03
