XRC Protocol
Specification

XRC — Xeevia Record Chain

A deterministic, off-chain verification protocol providing blockchain-grade traceability without blockchain cost or latency. Every event is cryptographically linked — producing immutable, query-optimized history for the entire Xeevia ecosystem.

Core Principles

Deterministic Truth

Every record is provable. No ambiguity. No trust required. Verify it yourself at any time.

Instant Retrieval

Verification under 5ms. Reads are free. Built to serve billions of queries without slowing down.

Modular Design

New chain types added without a protocol redesign. The architecture grows as the ecosystem grows.

Chain Architecture

XTRC

Transaction Chain

All value transfers, tips, gift cards, fees, withdrawals. Every satoshi accounted for.

XERC

Engagement Chain

Likes, comments, shares — weighted and verified. No synthetic signals pass through.

XARC

Account Chain

Signup, verification, device binding, security events. Your identity history, immutable.

XCRC

Creation, edits, ownership, deletion. Proof of authorship for everything you make.

System Chain

Protocol upgrades, rule changes, policy deployments. No hidden governance.
